The first step is fresh beans.


We only use organically-sourced, fair trade whole coffee beans for our variety of refreshing drinks. The fresher the bean, the better the drink.

Invest in a good grinder.


The size and shape of your coffee grounds matter. A blade grinder may not give the most consistent coffee grounds. Using a burr grinder is a better option.

Check weight & temperature.


It's best to use a scale to weigh you grounds and a measuring cup to check the amount of water and grounds according to the directions on the bag. Optimal temperature is between 195° and 205°.

Time it.


Using a timer on your cellphone should do just fine. Read the directions on the coffee's package to determine length to make sure you don't over-do your drink.
